# Account Login

Swift Craft Launcher supports two main login methods: Offline mode and Microsoft official login.

# Offline Mode

No network verification required. Enter any username to start playing. Only suitable for single-player mode or servers with online authentication disabled.

<div id="offline-step1" class="Task">

## Step 1: Add Player

<div id="aria-offline-step1" class="TaskBody" role="region" aria-hidden="false">

Click "Add Player" in the launcher.

<figure>
<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/suhang12332/Swift-Craft-Launcher-Assets/refs/heads/main/imagebed/register/1-1_1.png" alt="Add Player button">
</figure>

</div>

</div>

<div id="offline-step2" class="Task">

## Step 2: Select Offline Mode

<div id="aria-offline-step2" class="TaskBody" role="region" aria-hidden="false">

Select the "Offline" option in the top right corner, enter your desired player name, then click "Create Account".

<figure>
<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/suhang12332/Swift-Craft-Launcher-Assets/refs/heads/main/imagebed/register/1-1_2.png" alt="Offline mode selection">
</figure>

</div>

</div>

<div id="offline-step3" class="Task">

## Step 3: Confirm Addition

<div id="aria-offline-step3" class="TaskBody" role="region" aria-hidden="false">

When your player information appears in the bottom left corner of the launcher, the account has been successfully added.

<figure>
<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/suhang12332/Swift-Craft-Launcher-Assets/refs/heads/main/imagebed/register/1-1_3.png" alt="Account added successfully">
</figure>

</div>

</div>

# Microsoft Login

Authenticate using your official Microsoft account. This is the only way to access all official Minecraft features. You must have a purchased copy of Minecraft to use this method.

<div id="microsoft-step1" class="Task">

## Step 1: Add Player

<div id="aria-microsoft-step1" class="TaskBody" role="region" aria-hidden="false">

Click "Add Player" in the launcher.

<figure>
<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/suhang12332/Swift-Craft-Launcher-Assets/refs/heads/main/imagebed/register/1-1_1.png" alt="Add Player button">
</figure>

</div>

</div>

<div id="microsoft-step2" class="Task">

## Step 2: Select Microsoft Login

<div id="aria-microsoft-step2" class="TaskBody" role="region" aria-hidden="false">

Select the "Microsoft" option in the top right corner, then click "Start Login".

<figure>
<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/suhang12332/Swift-Craft-Launcher-Assets/refs/heads/main/imagebed/register/1-2_2.png" alt="Microsoft login option">
</figure>

</div>

</div>

<div id="microsoft-step3" class="Task">

## Step 3: Browser Login

<div id="aria-microsoft-step3" class="TaskBody" role="region" aria-hidden="false">

Click to allow opening the browser, then follow the instructions on the webpage to log in to your Microsoft account and authorize Swift Craft Launcher.

<figure>
<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/suhang12332/Swift-Craft-Launcher-Assets/refs/heads/main/imagebed/register/1-2_3.png" alt="Browser login">
</figure>

</div>

</div>

<div id="microsoft-step4" class="Task">

## Step 4: Confirm Addition

<div id="aria-microsoft-step4" class="TaskBody" role="region" aria-hidden="false">

Return to the launcher and confirm the addition.

<figure>
<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/suhang12332/Swift-Craft-Launcher-Assets/refs/heads/main/imagebed/register/1-2_4.png" alt="Confirm addition">
</figure>

</div>

</div>

<div id="microsoft-step5" class="Task">

## Step 5: Complete Login

<div id="aria-microsoft-step5" class="TaskBody" role="region" aria-hidden="false">

When your player information appears in the bottom left corner of the launcher, the account has been successfully added.

<figure>
<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/suhang12332/Swift-Craft-Launcher-Assets/refs/heads/main/imagebed/register/1-2_5.png" alt="Account added successfully">
</figure>

</div>

</div>
