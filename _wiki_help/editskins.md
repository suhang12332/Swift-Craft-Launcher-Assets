# Edit Skins

Swift Craft Launcher allows you to customize player skins, switch between skin models, and manage capes.

# Upload Skin

<div id="skin-step1" class="Task">

## Step 1: Open Skin Manager

<div id="aria-skin-step1" class="TaskBody" role="region" aria-hidden="false">

Click "Skin Manager" to open the skin management interface.

<figure>
<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/suhang12332/Swift-Craft-Launcher-Assets/refs/heads/main/imagebed/editskins/5_1.png" alt="Skin Manager">
</figure>

</div>

</div>

<div id="skin-step2" class="Task">

## Step 2: View Feature Options

<div id="aria-skin-step2" class="TaskBody" role="region" aria-hidden="false">

The Skin Manager provides several options: "Switch Skin Model", "Upload Local Skin", "Cape Modification", and "Reset to Default Skin".

<figure>
<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/suhang12332/Swift-Craft-Launcher-Assets/refs/heads/main/imagebed/editskins/5_2.png" alt="Skin Manager features">
</figure>

</div>

</div>

<div id="skin-step3" class="Task">

## Step 3: Select Skin File

<div id="aria-skin-step3" class="TaskBody" role="region" aria-hidden="false">

Click "Upload Skin", select a local skin file from your computer, then click "Open".

<figure>
<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/suhang12332/Swift-Craft-Launcher-Assets/refs/heads/main/imagebed/editskins/5_3.png" alt="Select skin file">
</figure>

</div>

</div>

<div id="skin-step4" class="Task">

## Step 4: Apply Changes

<div id="aria-skin-step4" class="TaskBody" role="region" aria-hidden="false">

After confirming everything is correct, click "Apply Changes" to save your new skin.

<figure>
<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/suhang12332/Swift-Craft-Launcher-Assets/refs/heads/main/imagebed/editskins/5_4.png" alt="Apply changes">
</figure>

</div>

</div>

<div id="skin-step5" class="Task">

## Step 5: Confirm Upload Success

<div id="aria-skin-step5" class="TaskBody" role="region" aria-hidden="false">

If the character avatar in the bottom left corner is replaced with your new skin, the upload was successful.

<figure>
<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/suhang12332/Swift-Craft-Launcher-Assets/refs/heads/main/imagebed/editskins/5_5.png" alt="Upload successful">
</figure>

</div>

</div>
