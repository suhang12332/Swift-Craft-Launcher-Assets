Account Login

Swift Craft Launcher supports three main login methods: Offline mode, Microsoft official login, and third-party authentication server login.

Offline Mode

No network verification required. Enter any username to start playing. Only suitable for single-player mode or servers with online authentication disabled.

Step 1: Add Player

Click "Add Player" in the launcher.

Step 2: Select Offline Mode

Select the "Offline" option in the top right corner, enter your desired player name, then click "Create Account".

Step 3: Confirm Addition

When your player information appears in the bottom left corner of the launcher, the account has been successfully added.

Microsoft Login

Authenticate using your official Microsoft account. This is the only way to access all official Minecraft features. You must have a purchased copy of Minecraft to use this method.

Step 1: Add Player

Click "Add Player" in the launcher.

Step 2: Select Microsoft Login

Select the "Microsoft" option in the top right corner, then click "Start Login".

Step 3: Browser Login

Click to allow opening the browser, then follow the instructions on the webpage to log in to your Microsoft account and authorize Swift Craft Launcher.

Step 4: Confirm Addition

Return to the launcher and confirm the addition.

Step 5: Complete Login

When your player information appears in the bottom left corner of the launcher, the account has been successfully added.

Third-Party Login

Sign in with a third-party Minecraft authentication server such as LittleSkin, MUA, or Ely.By. After login, you can choose from the roles returned by that server, and the launcher will automatically connect the correct authentication and skin service when the game starts.

Step 1: Add Player

Click "Add Player" in the launcher.

Step 2: Select a Third-Party Server

Switch to the "Third-Party" login type, choose a server preset, then click "Start Login".

The launcher currently includes presets for LittleSkin, MUA, and Ely.By.

Step 3: Browser Authorization

The launcher opens your browser and redirects you to the selected third-party server's authorization page. Follow the instructions there to sign in and authorize Swift Craft Launcher.

Step 4: Choose a Role

After authorization, the launcher automatically reads the list of roles under that account.

If the server returns multiple roles, you can switch between them in the interface and choose which one to add.

Step 5: Complete Addition

When the selected role appears in the bottom left corner of the launcher, the third-party account has been added successfully.

When launching the game, the launcher automatically injects the required authentication component so the game can use that server's authentication and skin service.
